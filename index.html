What we offer.

Welcome to the Hotel Bling, located in the heart of Chicago! Our hotel offers a wide range of services and amenities to make your stay as comfortable and convenient as possible. From spacious guest rooms with modern amenities to a variety of on-site dining options, Hotel Bling Hotel has something for everyone.

Our guest rooms offer a variety of features to ensure your comfort. Each room is equipped with a king or queen-size bed, contemporary furnishings, and a flatscreen television. Additionally, all guest rooms come with complimentary wireless internet access.

Hotel Bling also features an on-site fitness center with state-of-the-art equipment. Guests can enjoy a variety of cardiovascular and strength-training machines as well as free weights, all of which are available 24 hours a day.

For dining, Hotel Bling offers a variety of options. Our on-site restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. In addition, we have a coffee shop for quick snacks and drinks. We also offer room service for those who prefer to dine in the comfort of their guest room.

Hotel Bling also provides a variety of recreational activities for our guests. We have an outdoor pool and a hot tub, perfect for cooling off on hot summer days. We also have a game room with billiards, air hockey, and other games.
